# FMCG Sales, Marketing & Profitability Analysis

##  Project Objective
Analyze FMCG sales performance, profitability, marketing efficiency, customer behavior, and regional trends to identify business growth opportunities and support data-driven decision-making.

---

#  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description | Formula |
|---|---|---|
| **Total Gross Sales** | Total sales value before discounts and deductions | — |
| **Total Net Revenue** | Actual revenue generated after discounts and adjustments | — |
| **Total Profit** | Earnings after deducting all operational costs | — |
| **Profit Margin %** | Percentage of revenue converted into profit | `Profit / Net Revenue × 100` |
| **Total Units Sold** | Total quantity of products sold | — |
| **Marketing ROI %** | Profitability generated from marketing investment | `(Profit − Marketing Spend) / Marketing Spend × 100` |
| **YoY Revenue Growth %** | Year-over-year revenue growth | `(Current Year Revenue − Previous Year Revenue) / Previous Year Revenue × 100` |
| **YoY Profit Growth %** | Year-over-year profit growth | `(Current Year Profit − Previous Year Profit) / Previous Year Profit × 100` |

#  Business Questions Addressed

### Sales & Profitability Analysis
- Which products and categories generate the highest sales and profit?
- Which products generate high sales but low profit?
- How do discounts impact profit margins?

### Regional Performance Analysis
- Which regions and countries perform best in revenue and profitability?
- Which geographic markets show the highest growth potential?

### Growth Trend Analysis
- How has business growth changed from 2023 to 2025?
- What are the YoY revenue and profit growth trends?

### Customer & Channel Analysis
- Which sales channels contribute the most revenue?
- Which customer segments drive maximum profitability?

### Marketing Efficiency Analysis
- How effective are marketing campaigns in generating ROI?
- Which strategic areas deserve higher investment?

### Operational Efficiency Analysis
- Which operational costs are reducing profitability?
- What opportunities exist to improve business performance?
