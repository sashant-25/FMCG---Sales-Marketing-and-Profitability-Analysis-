# FMCG---Sales-Marketing-and-Profitability-Analysis-

Objective: Analyze FMCG sales, profitability, marketing efficiency, customer segments, and regional performance to identify business growth opportunities.

Tools Used: 
      - Microsoft Excel
      - Pivot Tables
      - Power Query
      - Pivot Charts
      - Conditional Formatting
      - Power BI

KPIs Analyzed: 
      - Total Gross Sales: Total sales value before discounts and deductions.
      - Total Net Revenue: Actual revenue generated after discounts and adjustments.
      - Total Profit: Total earnings after deducting all operational costs.
      - Profit Margin %: Percentage of revenue converted into profit.  [𝑃𝑟𝑜𝑓𝑖𝑡 𝑀𝑎𝑟𝑔𝑖𝑛 % =  𝑃𝑟𝑜𝑓𝑖𝑡/(𝑁𝑒𝑡 𝑅𝑒𝑣𝑒𝑛𝑢𝑒)  × 100]
      - Total Units Sold: Total quantity of products sold.
      - Marketing ROI %: Measures profitability generated from marketing investment.  [𝑀𝑎𝑟𝑘𝑒𝑡𝑖𝑛𝑔 𝑅𝑂𝐼 % =  (𝑃𝑟𝑜𝑓𝑖𝑡 −𝑀𝑎𝑟𝑘𝑒𝑡𝑖𝑛𝑔 𝑆𝑝𝑒𝑛𝑑)/(𝑀𝑎𝑟𝑘𝑒𝑡𝑖𝑛𝑔 𝑆𝑝𝑒𝑛𝑑)  × 100]
      - YoY Revenue Growth %: Shows how revenue changed year-over-year.
      - YoY Profit Growth %: Shows business profitability growth trend.

Business Questions Addressed:
      1. Which products and categories generate the highest sales and profit?
      2. Which regions and countries perform best in terms of revenue and profitability?
      3. How has business growth changed from 2023 to 2025?
      4. Which sales channels and customer segments contribute the most revenue?
      5. How effective are marketing campaigns in generating ROI?
      6. How do discounts impact profit margins?
      7. Which operational costs are reducing profitability?
      8. Which products generate high sales but low profit?
      9. What opportunities exist for improving business performance and profitability?
      10. Which strategic areas should receive higher business investment?
